# Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)
## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze business performance by examining sales, profit, and product demand across regions, states, and product categories. The dashboard helps identify key trends and supports data-driven decision making for improving sales performance and profitability.

## Dataset Used

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Sales: 2.30M
- Total Profit: 286.40K
- Total Quantity Sold: 38K
- Additional metrics include:
- Sales & Profit trend by year
- Profit by region
- Sales by product sub-category
- State-wise sales distribution
## Project Process
### Data Collection
The dataset contains order-level sales information including sales, profit, quantity, region, state, and product sub-categories.
### Data Cleaning & Preparation
The dataset was preprocessed to ensure data quality before analysis.
#### Steps performed:
-Removed null and missing values
-Checked for duplicate records
- Verified data types and formatting
- Ensured consistency across categorical variables
- The dataset was already relatively clean, so only minor data cleaning and validation were required.
#### 3Dashboard Development
- The cleaned data was imported into Power BI, and multiple visualizations were created to highlight business performance:
- Donut chart for quantity by region
- Line chart for yearly sales and profit trends
- KPI cards for total metrics
- Funnel chart for sales by sub-category
- Map visualization for state-wise sales
- Detailed table for product performance
- State slicer for interactive filtering

 ## Project Insights
- The West region generates the highest profit, while the Central region shows lower profitability.
- Sales and profit increased steadily from 2014 to 2017, with the highest sales recorded in 2017.
- Phones, Chairs, and Storage are among the top-performing product sub-categories.
- Some sub-categories generate high sales but lower profit margins, indicating potential pricing or cost optimization opportunities.

## Conclusion
The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of business performance across regions and product categories. It helps stakeholders quickly identify sales trends, profitable products, and regional performance, enabling better strategic and operational decisions.
